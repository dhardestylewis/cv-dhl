# Interfere - Founding AI Engineer (S25)
https://www.workatastartup.com/jobs/86526

Interfere is building the self-healing layer of the internet: software that detects issues, diagnoses root causes, and ships fixes before anyone notices something's broken. 

## The Moment
AI has democratized shipping code, but excellence remains scarce. We're creating the infrastructure that makes product excellence no longer aspirational but inevitable and expected. We've raised over $5.1M from Y Combinator, Vercel Ventures, Hummingbird, Designer Fund.

## The Role
As a Founding AI Engineer, you'll build the intelligence that powers autonomous software repair. You'll design and train systems that understand codebases, reason about failures, and generate fixes that work. This is applied AI at its most consequential - where model performance directly determines whether production systems stay online.

## What You'll Build
- Detection systems that identify anomalies and failures across millions of codebases
- Diagnostic models that trace bugs to root causes through complex execution paths
- Code generation systems that produce fixes developers trust to ship
- Reasoning engines that understand when to act autonomously and when to defer to humans
- Evaluation frameworks that measure what matters - real-world reliability, not benchmark scores
- Infrastructure that serves AI models at scale with latency that doesn't break user experience

## What We're Looking For
- Immense pride in the work you do
- Builders with taste: You know the difference between good and great
- Systems thinkers: You see patterns others miss, and you understand how AI fits into larger systems. You design for reliability, not just capability
- Relentless learners: You dive into unfamiliar domains and emerge with expertise
- Domain experts: Deep experience with LLMs, prompt engineering, fine-tuning, or AI infrastructure. You've shipped AI systems that real users depend on.

## Nice to Have
- Experience with code analysis, program synthesis, or software engineering AI agents
- Track record with RAG systems, agentic workflows, or multi-step reasoning
- Deep familiarity with model serving infrastructure, vector databases, or real-time inference
- Background in developer tools, observability platforms, or infrastructure
