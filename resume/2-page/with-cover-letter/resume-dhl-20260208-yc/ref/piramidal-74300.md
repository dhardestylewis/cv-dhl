# Piramidal - Research Engineer, Foundational AI (W24)
https://www.workatastartup.com/jobs/74300

We are a neuro first company. As a Research Engineer here, you will build AI systems focused on neural data, capable of accomplishing previously impossible tasks. We're seeking individuals with strong engineering skills, including designing, implementing, and enhancing massive-scale distributed machine learning systems, producing bug-free machine learning code, and contributing to the neuroscience underlying algorithmic innovations.

Outstanding deep learning achievements increasingly depend on massive-scale systems, requiring engineers comfortable working in large distributed systems.

## We expect you to have:
- Bachelor's degree in computer science, mathematics, or physics.
- 4+ years of software engineering experience.
- 4+ years of experience with deep learning libraries (e.g., PyTorch, JAX, or Julia).
- Experience working in large distributed systems.
- Enthusiasm for AI Symbiosis.

## Nice to have:
- Master's or PhD in statistics or a related field.
- Experience in dense time series and/or other continuous data types.
- Experience in signal processing.
- Past experience in creating high-performance implementations of deep learning algorithms.

You do not need to have experience in neuroscience.

## About Piramidal
We are building a first-of-its-kind foundation model for electrophysiological brain data. Our goal is to create scaled neural decoders that enable humans to understand and control neural syntax.
