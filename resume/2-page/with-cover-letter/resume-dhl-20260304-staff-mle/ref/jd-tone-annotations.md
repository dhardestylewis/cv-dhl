# JD Tone Annotations

## Photonium (X25)
**Tone:** Direct, practical, no-nonsense. Short bullets, no fluff.
**Key phrases to mirror:**
- "You'll develop ML models"
- "Build workflows to interface with"
- "Collaborate with optical engineers"
- "Interest in optics, physics-based simulation, or generative design"

**Vibe:** Engineer talking to engineer. Just tell me what you can do.

## Interfere (S25)
**Tone:** Intense, poetic, high-energy founder energy. Mission-driven manifesto.
**Key phrases to mirror:**
- "Difficulty wakes the genius"
- "the intersection of the impossible and the inevitable"
- "can't sleep until you've achieved the latter"
- "Ownership without borders. No excuses."
- "We value proof of who you can be over who you've been"
- "Send us evidence of exceptional work you've shipped"

**Vibe:** Show intensity. This isn't a job, it's a calling. Match that energy.

**Tone:** Blunt, casual, no-BS startup culture. Direct and honest.

- "Doers, not yappers, function over form"

**Vibe:** They're tired of BS. Be direct. Show don't tell. Function over form.
